The Process

Though the process is evolving with emerging technology and differs depending on the tools used, the steps taken to archive a website are fairly consistent. You may not see all these steps in action if you’re using a browser plugin, for example, but knowing what’s going on will lead to a deeper understanding of the process. This in turn will lead to better selection of tools, easier to provide access and reference to those wanting to see what you’ve saved.
