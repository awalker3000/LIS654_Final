Web Archiving Projects

See projects below.

NYARC is made up of the research libraries of the Brooklyn Museum, the Frick Collection, and the Museum of Modern Art. Together, they run an ambitious web archiving program to collect, preserve and provide access to ten curated collections of websites that compliment each library’s print holdings as well as the archived websites of their parent institutions.

The NYARC web resources program collects 3,933 websites in the following ten categories:

  • Art Resources
  • Artists’ Websites
  • Auction Houses
  • Brooklyn Museum
  • Catalogues Raisonnés
  • Museum of Modern Art 
  • New York Art Resources Consortium (NYARC)
  • New York Galleries
  • Restitution of Lost and Looted Art
  • The Frick Collection
